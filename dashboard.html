Applying Strategy

Don't wait for them to find you. Be proactive.

  • Find Contacts: Use Apollo.io or Hunter.io to find Engineering Managers, CTOs, or Founders. Avoid HR if possible.
  • Setup Email: Register a professional domain (e.g., firstname.dev) to ensure high deliverability and professional appearance.
  • Cold Email Template: Keep it short and value-driven.
    "Hi [Name], I'm a Backend Dev who loves what you're doing at [Company]. I recently built [Project] using [Tech Stack] and would love to bring this experience to your team. Let me know if you have 10 mins to chat. Here is my GitHub: [Link]."
  • Follow Up: Send a polite follow-up after 3 days if no response. Persistence is key.
